Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a crispy crust, tomato sauce, and various toppings. It is a versatile meal that can be customized to suit any taste preferences.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Add your desired toppings.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
