Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It is a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned.
  3. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 15 minutes.
  6. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  7. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  8. Spread a thin layer of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  9. Layer half of the noodles, then half of the ricotta cheese, and half of the mozzarella cheese.
  10. Spread a layer of sauce over the cheese.
  11. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of sauce and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.
  12. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown.
